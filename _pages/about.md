My broad area of research is Development Economics. I work on socio-economic inequality, including income, consumption, and education. My work explores the stratification-economics, particularly caste-stratification in India. My interest lies in both inequality of outcome and opportunity. 
I am also interested in climate change and its effect on inequality. I really enjoy working with applied econometrics, causal inference, machine learning, and large datasets, such as panel household surveys, nighttime lights, and gridded data.
